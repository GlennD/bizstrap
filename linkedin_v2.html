Cost-per-click (CPC) and cost-per-thousand-impressions (CPM) are 2 different styles of billing you can choose for ads.

  • CPC - You are charged up to your maximum bid each time your ad is clicked by a member of your target audience.
  • CPM - You are charged up to your maximum bid each time your ad is shown 1,000 times.
