Retarget Site Visitors

Most prospects don't convert on their first visit to your website (or second, or third...). Retargeting lets you re-acquire and convert your lost visitors, maximizing the results of all of your marketing dollars.

Amplify Social Marketing

The Bizo platform allows you to share links and engage your social media followers with Bizo Social Marketing, then amplify your social efforts by retargeting shared link clickers with display advertisements.

Low-risk CPC Pricing

  • Bizo Retargeting uses a cost per click model. We maximize clicks while minimizing CPC-getting the most for your budget.
  • Average campaigns run from $1.00-$2.50 CPC, with many under $1.00.
  • No matter how your campaign performs, Bizo will never charge more than $10 per click.
