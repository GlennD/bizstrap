Audience Management Advanced analytics for deeper audience understanding

• Partition your site into segments to understand how traffic differs by segment.

• Understand which audiences are performing key actions on your site.

• Use actions to measure campaign success and tailor content to the audience.

Action Tracking

Action tracking allows you to gain clear visibility into the Bizographic profiles of the audiences who are performing key actions on your site such as "Whitepaper Downloads" or "Event Registrations." Create an action by giving it a name and specifying a URL that marks the completion of the action.

Action metrics are displayed in your Audience Analysis reports. Once you understand the profiles of your converting audience you can leverage Bizo marketing campaigns to bring more of your target audience to your site.

Measure how your retargeting campaigns drive conversions on your site by including an action in your Retargeting campaigns.

Campaign optimization with Actions

  1. Create an action that represents a successful transaction you want to drive on your site.
  2. Launch a Retargeting campaign to draw in your desired audience.
  3. View the action's conversion metrics on the Trends report to measure the campaign's impact.

Custom Segments

Custom segments allow you to identify visitors who view specific areas of your site. Once defined, a custom segment can be viewed within the Audience Insight report which will display the Bizographic profiles of visitors to this area of your site.

Like actions, custom segment metrics are displayed in your Audience Analysis reports.

Personalized Retargeting with Custom Segments

  1. Create a custom segment for a distinct part of your site (a section about a particular product, for example).
  2. The Bizo Insight tags on your segment pages will track visitors to that segment.
  3. Launch a Retargeting display campaign to draw those visitors back to your site and re-engage them.
