Easy Share

Connected: @bizo

Install Easy Share to Chrome

Display your bookmarks bar (if hidden) by clicking Wrench Icon > Bookmarks > Show Bookmarks Bar

Then, drag this button up into your bookmarks bar. Bizo Easy Share

How to use

Sharing Links

  1. Click on Bizo Easy Share in your bookmarks bar to launch the EasyShare bookmarklet.
  2. The URL will be automatically shortened. Click save to add it to your SmartLinks report, or add a message and publish it to Twitter.

Brand Bar

When sharing third-party content, click the Brand Bar button to keep your brand top of mind and drive leads.

Leave the Brand Bar off when sharing links to your own websites—your audience can already engage with you there.

Promoting Shares

When sharing links to your own content, click the Promote button to turn the share into an ad unit that will display in your Brand Bar.

Conversion Tracking

Add Actions to your shares to track conversions from shared links—down to the tweet level—for deeper insight into social ROI. Track the bizographic profiles of the people converting from your shared links on the Trends report.
