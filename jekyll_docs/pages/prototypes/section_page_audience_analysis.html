Audience Analytics Deep and actionable audience insights

• Find out if your target audience is visiting and converting on your website

• Uncover potentially untapped audiences that are worth targeting

• Inform the creation of relevant website content

Audience Insight

Gain clear visibility into the business demographic or "bizographic" makeup of your website visitors:

  • Company Size - From Micro (1-20 employees) up to Fortune 500
  • Industry - Granular industry demographic tracking (e.g., Finance, Software, Healthcare)
  • Functional Area - A business professional's role in the organization (e.g., Sales, Legal, IT)
  • Seniority - From non-managers to mid-management to executives
  • Location - All US states, Canada and the UK

View Insight Report

Trends

Examine the bizographic trends of your website traffic over time with page view, visit, and action tracking metrics. Customize your view to show multiple audience trends together in one graph. Slice results by domain, or set up custom audience segments for greater precision.

View Trends Report

Biz Index

See how the bizographics of your audience compare to a typical site on the Bizo network. Is your site visited by an unusually high number of HR professionals? Maybe you get more visits from medium-size businesses than most sites. Use these insights to influence your content strategy.

View Biz Index Report

How to use Audience Analysis

  1. Make sure to tag your sites with the Bizo Insight tag. This tag will start collecting data on the users visiting your site.
  2. Analyze the Audience Insight, Trends, and Biz Index reports to get a deep understanding of your audience.
  3. Set up custom audience segments to identify visitors to specific areas of your site. You're now ready to launch a retargeting campaign to target those visitors.
