Easy Share

Connected: @bizo

Install Easy Share to Chrome

Display your bookmarks bar (if hidden) by clicking Wrench Icon > Bookmarks > Show Bookmarks Bar

Then, install the bookmarklet by dragging this button up into your bookmarks bar.

How to Use

Click on Easy Share in your bookmarks bar to share the page you're viewing with your social followers.

The URL will be automatically shortened and added to your SmartLinks report.

Features

Conversion Tracking

Add Actions to your shares to track conversions from shared links—down to the tweet level—for deeper insight into social ROI. Track the bizographic profiles of the people converting from your shared links.

Brand Bar

When sharing third-party content, enable the Brand Bar to keep your brand top of mind and drive leads.

Tip: Leave the Brand Bar off when sharing links to your own websites—your audience will already be able to engage with you there.

Tagging

Create tags for your campaigns or products while sharing for organization and analysis.
