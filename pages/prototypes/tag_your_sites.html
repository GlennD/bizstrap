Tag Your Websites

Why is this so important?

Insight Tags read the bizographic data of your web audiences and connect your websites to the Bizo Marketing Platform.

Step 1 Apply Insight Tags to your websites

Add the following javascript code to every page on your websites, preferably right before the end of the <body> tag:

<script type="text/javascript">
  _bizo_data_partner_id = "14";
</script>
<script type="text/javascript">
(function(){var b,a,u;a=document.createElement("script");a.type="text/javascript";a.async=true;u=(window.location.protocol==="https:"?"https://sjs.":"http://js.")+"bizographics.com/insight.min.js";a.src=u;b=document.getElementsByTagName("script")[0];return b.parentNode.insertBefore(a,b)})();
</script>

Depending on your site infrastructure, you may want to make the tag as a standard part of your page template or include it in your content management system to make sure it's on every page.

A couple of things to remember:

  • The Insight Tag automatically handles secure and non-secure pages. There is no need for separate code snippets.
  • The Insight Tag cannot be delivered from within an iframe, it must be applied to the page itself.

Step 2 Add tagged websites to your account

Once a site has been tagged, add its domain to your list of websites below (we'd enter "bizo.com" for ours). Within an hour, you will see a validation message next to your domains in the table below.

You'll be able to view your Audience Analytics 24 hours after tagging.
