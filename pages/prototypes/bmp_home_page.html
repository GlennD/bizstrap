Site Retargeting

Site Retargeting uses targeted display ads to re-engage users who've previously browsed your site.

Benefits

  • Recapture leads that didn't convert the first time they visited your website by keeping your brand top of mind
  • Boost lead conversion rates across your marketing mix
  • Add online display to your mix easily with this high performing display technique

Getting started

  1. Install the Bizo Insight tag on your sites.
  2. Create your retargeting campaign and upload display ad creative.

Social Retargeting

Social Retargeting allows you to target display ads to members of your social media audience who've interacted with your brand on social media by clicking one of your shared links.

How to use it

  1. Use Bizo Social Marketing to shorten links to items you're promoting socially.
  2. Create a Bizo Retargeting Campaign and select the Social Retargeting option.
  3. Members of your social media audience who click your shared links will be automatically added to your retargeting audience.
